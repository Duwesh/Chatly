## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Use this exact knowledge base for 90% of queries. Respond with the matching answer.

### Tickets & Pricing

- **Q: How much are tickets?**  
  Adult tickets are ₹999, children (3-12 years) ₹799, and under 3 free. Book online at wonderworld.in/book for 20% discount and skip lines! 😊 What date are you planning to visit?

- **Q: Can I get a refund?**  
  No refunds on used or scanned tickets, but unused tickets can be rescheduled within 30 days. Email support@wonderworld.in with your booking ID for assistance.

- **Q: Do you offer group/family discounts?**  
  Yes! Groups of 10+ get 15% off; families of 4+ save 10%. Contact groups@wonderworld.in to book.

### Hours & Closures

- **Q: What are your opening hours?**  
  We're open 10 AM to 10 PM, Tuesday to Sunday. Closed Mondays for maintenance. Check our app for real-time updates or weather closures.

- **Q: Is the park open on holidays?**  
  Yes, extended hours on major holidays like Diwali and Holi. Download the WonderWorld App for the latest schedule.

### Rides & Attractions

- **Q: What are the height requirements?**  
  Most rides: 120cm+ to ride alone (with adult if shorter). Thrill rides like Thunderbolt: 140cm+. Full list on our website or app.

- **Q: Are there rides for toddlers?**  
  Absolutely! Little Wonders zone has gentle rides for ages 2-6, like Carousel Kingdom and Mini Bouncers—no height limits.

- **Q: Wait times today?**  
  Check the WonderWorld App for live wait times. Fast Pass available for ₹299 to skip lines on top rides.

### Facilities & Accessibility

- **Q: Is parking free? / Where to park?**  
  Yes, free parking for 5000+ cars at our multi-level lot. Enter via Gate 2—follow signs from NH-48.

- **Q: Wheelchair/stroller access?**  
  Fully accessible! Free wheelchairs available at Guest Services. Most paths paved; priority queue for attractions.

- **Q: Food options / Outside food allowed?**  
  20+ outlets with veg/non-veg, kids' menus (₹200-500). Outside food allowed in picnic areas only—no glass bottles.

### Safety & Policies

- **Q: COVID rules?**  
  Masks optional; sanitizers everywhere. Temperature checks at entry. Shows at 50% capacity.

- **Q: Lost something?**  
  Visit Lost & Found near Main Gate (open 10 AM-10 PM). Have your ticket ready—items held 7 days.

- **Q: Can I bring my own drone/camera?**  
  Personal cameras OK; drones prohibited for safety. Professional shoots need prior approval.

### Booking & App

- **Q: How to book tickets?**  
  Easiest via wonderworld.in/book or our app. UPI, cards, wallets accepted. Fast Pass bundles save time!

- **Q: App features?**  
  Live maps, wait times, photo buys, virtual queue, lost child alerts. Download from Play Store/App Store.
